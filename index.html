The Problem

Women and non-binary individuals are woefully underrepresented as speakers at professional technology events as shown by recent research in Washington, DC and Austin. This lack of representation can have far-reaching effects.

  • Marginalized groups miss out on opportunities to be presented as leaders or experts in their fields.
  • They are less likely to feel welcome to such groups, missing out on valuable learning and networking opportunities.

We want everyone to know that we speak too.

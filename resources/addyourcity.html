History

This project took shape over a weekend in Washington, DC. Unfortunately, the gender speaker gap is in no way limited to DC. The research and website are both open source and easily reproducible for your city. The most important element is local advocate(s) to engage the city's tech community. If you think that's you, read on.

Collect

Annotate the meetup speaker genders from your city's dataset! We created an awesome tool to help you collect it and publish it to data.world.

Tell the Story

Once you have a dataset, you can visualize it. You can fork the iframes Kate created for her original analysis by pointing them to your own dataset.

You can also use a tool like Tableau Public to create a public visualization to embed into your writeup.

Publish!

Add your work to the WeSpeakToo site!

  • Fork the most current repository.
  • Add your city's page, story, and JSON file for the Speaker List.
  • Submit a pull request!

Share

Tell local meetup organizers what you found. Let them know how they can help!
